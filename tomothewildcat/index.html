About the game

Tomo The Wildcat is a brand-new game inspired by Azumanga Daioh and Sonic Generations. In this game, you will run through lots of stages inspired on the Azumanga Daioh locations, such as an High School, Beach, Sports Camp, etc.

Features

Multiple playable characters

Play as all the main girls, each one with their own abilities! Tomo, Kagura, Osaka, Chiyo, Sakaki and Yomi.

Lots of content

This game features 8 main levels, 10 side-levels, and 1 secret level, giving a total of 19 levels. Plus 2 extra game modes give lots of replay value.

Video cutscenes

The story is driven by 3d and 2d fully animated cutscenes, with dialogue and some voice acting.

Modding support

You can very easily make your own playable character for the game, download other's and share your own on the internet!


Game Modes

  • Main Story: 18 levels, 8 bosses and multiple game styles.
  • Boss Rush: Fight against every boss in the game in order.
  • Tour Mode: Play every level in order without dying.

Community

You can join our community "AzuGames Daioh!" on our Discord server here. you can talk with other people, share or download mods, compare level times, etc. Have fun!


Or if you're not too much into that, feel free to download curated mods on the modding page, and upload your times to Youtube or Twitter tagging @LukasATBK or using the #TomoTheWildcat hashtag.
