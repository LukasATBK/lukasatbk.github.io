Requierements

List of programs you'll need to make a mod.

Aseprite (Paid)


Pixelart program needed for opening .ase and other image files.
Buy Aseprite (Website) | Buy Aseprite (Steam)

Libresprite


Free and open source alternative to Aseprite, pretty much the same.
Download Libresprite

*Note that you can follow the same steps on Libresprite and Aseprite, there's no notable differences

Optional:

Audacity


Free and open source audio editor, needed for editing the Level Complete music.
Download Audacity

*PSA: When downloading Audacity, click on "Download without Muse Hub" to ONLY download Audacity
